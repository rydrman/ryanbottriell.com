builderGame

I have always had a special love for 2d management and building games. Before going into my final year of University, I started a project to see if I could build a viable game of this genre in the web browser using HTML5 canvas drawing. I didn't get to much farther than some AI which build walls, but feel free to check it out nontheless.


Use the midle mouse button to click and drag the camera as well as zooming in and out.
