Feed the People is a strategic puzzle game where you have to manipulate the elements given to you in order to drop the fruit safely to the people!


Chose from four different level packs! More packs and levels coming soon! 


Contribute your score to the global score to raise money for the Charity STOP HUNGER NOW! This charity provides food packages to adults and children in need around the world. 


Features:

- Choose from four different level packs each containing three levels with more on the way!  

- Use up to four elements in a level to manipulate the surroundings and get the food to the people! 

- Contribute your score to help real people around the world! 

- Beat all our levels? Don't worry, enjoy free updates for more levels!
