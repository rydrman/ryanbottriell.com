Feed The People

In 2012 a group of my peers and I built this game for the Microsoft Imagine Cup. The Imagine Cup is and international competiton with different sections, one of which being game design.


Feed the People is a physics-based game built in XNA for the Windows Phone 7 platform. The idea was that through advertising revenues, players' time in the game could be turned into real charitable donations. Feed the People was designed to challenge its players as a global community to reach global score milestones. When milestones are reached, a lump sum would be donated to a charity involved with feeding the hungry.


Feed the People won second place in the canadian national finals of the Imagine Cup 2012.


Feed the People can be found on the Windows Phone app store here: Feed the People. Due to lack of resources, the advertising and donation features do not exist in the published version.
